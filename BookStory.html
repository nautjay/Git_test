Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New scientist, and all the other
scientific journals her work has appeared in, was a well thumbed copy of On the Road. It had been Molly's favorite book since college, and the longer she
spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last 10 years in this room, sitting under a poster with an Oscar Wilde quote proclaiming that Work is the refuge of people who have
nothing better to do. Although many considered her pionering work, unravelling the secrets of the llama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly,
did think she had something better to do.

Recipe For Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingridients
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream
Method
  1. Melt butter in a frying pan in a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making omelette)
  5. When eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
